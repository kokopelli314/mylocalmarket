Advantages of Local Produce

  • Most farmers travel less than 25 miles to sell at farmers markets, letting you bring fresher food to the table.
  • Buying directly from farmers means your money goes to the people making the food, not big supermarket chains.
  • Produce generally costs less at farmers markets than at grocery stores, according to a number of studies.
